# PRXshot PSP

PRXshot captures screenshots without pausing the game. This makes it particularly useful during online play, where pausing would interrupt the session. Each screenshot is saved and organized by game name and icon, so your captures are easy to find afterward.

**Runlevel:** ALWAYS, or any other runlevel.
